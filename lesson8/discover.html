About Enterprise City and Surrounding Areas

A rural farming community located in south west Utah, including the communities of Enterprise City, Newcastle, and Central. We are known for producing the best hay, corn and kids.

Last Visit:

Enterprise Rodeo is a highlight every 24th of July weekend - part of the Pioneer Day Celebrations.

Heritage Hall, built in 1913 was the second meetinghouse and school. In 1957, church services were moved and this building becamse Enterprise Heritage Hall.

Newcastle Resevoir and Enterprise Resevoir both provide fishing and camping for family fun.

Old Iron Town Historic Site, is the site of Iron City and the Union Iron Company, from 1868-1877.

Daughters of Utah Pioneers Museum is housed in the building that was originally the first meetinghouse used for both school and church meetings.

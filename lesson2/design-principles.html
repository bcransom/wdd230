Repetition

Apple
apple.com

Apple provides a good example of the principle of repetition, by repeating the same format for each product listed on their homepage. This is good as it helps users know right where they can find specific information for the product they are interested in.

Jen Simmons
labs.jensimmons.com

Jen Simmons has created a portfolio page full of coding examples. Her page shows the design principle proximity as she has grouped categories together creating a relationship between the different samples, and to provide ease of use to the user.

Membership Levels

Non-Profit Membership - Free

Free for non-profit organizations. Join today and get the exposure, networking and support you need.

Bronze Membership - $750

Perfect for smaller businesses with 30-50 employees. Two free tickets to the monthly Meet and Greet luncheon. Link to your website & social media pages on the Chamber online directory.

Silver Membership - $1000

Great for medium size businesses with 60-100 employees. Four free tickets to the monthly Meet and Greet luncheon. Link to your website & social media pages on the Chamber online directory. Advertisement on Chamber website "Spotlight" section.

Gold Membership - $1300

Great for medium size businesses with 60-100 employees. 6 free tickets to the monthly Meet and Greet luncheon. Link to your website & social media pages on the Chamber online directory. Advertisement on Chamber website "Spotlight" section. Free access to trainings held monthly. Free Ribbon Cutting, Open House and Ground Breaking support services.

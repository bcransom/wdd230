News Release

The Enterprise Annual Christmas Tree Lighting Ceremony will be on the first Monday in December this year. Enjoy hot chocolate and cookies, music provided by the community choir, and a visit from Santa.

Every year we also have a community Giving Tree, where you are able to donate to families in need for the holiday season. Tags, describing the age and need of a local child will be hanging on the tree, and will be available to select after the lighting ceremony. Donations of cash are also accepted to go toward a Christmas dinner for a family in need.

Remember that voting on the best home display for the Christmas Light Contest will begin the third week of December. All votes must be in by December 16th, as results will be announced on December 19th.

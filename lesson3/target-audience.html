Target Audience

Description

Business owners, professionals, community members and tourists.

Personas

Persona 1

Business owner, member of the chamber, ages 25-45.

Persona 2

Community member, does not have membership in chamber, ages 25-45

Scenarios

Scenario 1

Professional community member, looking to network with other professionals.

Scenario 2

Visitor to the community looking to find a list of businesses and their locations to help them plan their visit.

Scenario 3

Business owner and member of chamber, looking for feedback on current local economy.

Scenario 4

Local charitable organization looking to partner with other businesses in the community to host and fund a charitable dinner and auction.

Scenario 5

Professional and member of chamber, looking to attend seminar on business issues in the community.

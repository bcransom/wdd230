Scenarios

Scenario 1

Bill Gubler is looking for the next chamber event to attend. He wants to spend time talking with other members to get their feedback on the recent flooding that occurred due to heavy rains. He feels the city should spend more money to fix the infrastructure so flooding doesn’t occur again. He is hoping to find other community members who agree with his proposal, before he presents it to the city council next month. He visits the site and is able to find the calendar of events listing a luncheon for next week.

Scenario 2

Bill Gubler is wants to increase revenue at his Cafe, but isn’t sure how to do so. He visits the site to see if the Chamber has any helpful information to help him. He finds that the Chamber is hosting a seminar on advertising and marketing and decides to attend.

Scenario 3

Holly Wallace is busy planning a Christmas Raffle fundraiser for the local elementary school. She is looking for a list of local businesses that she can contact who may be willing to donate goods for the raffle. She visits the site and finds a list of businesses in the Directory.

Scenario 4

Holly Wallace wants to get the word out to as many people as she can that she is available to help with their real estate needs. She isn't sure of the best way to do this. She visits the site to see what options are available to her. She sees that there is a meet and greet luncheon coming up and decides to go in hopes of networking.

Scenario 5

Holly Wallace attended the meet and greet luncheon and was happy with her networking opportunity. She is interested in joining the Chamber as she feels it will be beneficial to her to have more opportunities to network for her real estate career, but also to build relationships in her capacity as PTA president. She has visited the site to look at the different membership options, and chooses one that fits her needs of networking and community support.
